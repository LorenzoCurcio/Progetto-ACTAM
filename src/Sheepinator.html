Challenge Mode
You can move the sheperd using WASD, Make sure you find the best position for listening to the angelic chant of the sheeps.
Also please be silent, we don't want to scare them right?
Have fun!
In "Challenge Mode" the sheeps must eat enough healthy grass in the field before the wolf comes.
Each time they eat, the mode in which they sing is switched to a happier one, you can move the sheeps by scaring them.
When they're full, click the Home button and they'll be back in a safe place.
